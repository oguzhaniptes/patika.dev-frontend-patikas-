Liver Saute

Liver kebab originates from Diyarbakır, Gaziantep, and Şanlıurfa, but is frequently found in the southern provinces of Turkey. a type of kebab. It is also served as breakfast in the mornings in Gaziantep, Şanlıurfa and Diyarbakır. consumption is present.

For how many person: 4-6

Preparation time: 5 min.

Cooking time: 30 min.

Ingredients for Liver Sauteed Recipe

  • 500 gr Liver
  • 1 red papper
  • 2 green pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomato
  • black pepper, chili pepper, salt
  • frying oil

How to Make Liver Saute?

  1. Chop the liver. Chop the onions into rings. Chop the red pepper, green pepper, tomatoes into cubes.
  2. Saute the onions in oil, add the peppers when they turn pink.
  3. When the peppers change color, add the livers, spices and salt.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes and cook.

Enjoy your meal
